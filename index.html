🔎 How this was built

Five subagents ran in parallel, each on one data source, returning only signals (places, work clues, lifestyle) — never raw private content.

🏗️ Property prices & monthly payments

Each place-to-live card lists multiple real, currently-marketed projects from real developers (researched by 5 subagents against 2025–26 listings) — brand, total price, and an estimated monthly amortization, spanning budget → premium per area, including the new growth estates (LIMA, New Clark City, Subic).

Monthly = standard basis: 20% down · 80% financed · 20-year term · 7.0% p.a. (≈₱7,753/mo per ₱1M financed — representative PH bank fix; Pag-IBIG can be lower). The "% of your ₱120k/mo savings" badge shows how heavy the amortization is against your save capacity. Foreign markets (Singapore ABSD 60% / no HDB; Bangkok 49% freehold quota) are flagged and their monthlies are illustrative since foreigners face restricted local financing. All figures are 2026 estimates, not quotes.

How scoring works

Every candidate is rated 0–100 on each criterion (seeded from your signals). Your priority weights produce a weighted average. Change a weight and the weighted average — and the ranking — moves. No candidate is "objectively" best; the ranking is best fit for what you said matters.

Compass is a thinking aid, not an oracle. It makes your trade-offs explicit. The final call is yours.
