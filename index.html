🔎 How this was built

Five subagents ran in parallel, each on one data source, returning only signals (places, work clues, lifestyle) — never raw private content.

How scoring works

Every candidate is rated 0–100 on each criterion (seeded from your signals). Your priority weights produce a weighted average. Change a weight and the weighted average — and the ranking — moves. No candidate is "objectively" best; the ranking is best fit for what you said matters.

Compass is a thinking aid, not an oracle. It makes your trade-offs explicit. The final call is yours.

Your data, your device

Everything lives in this browser's localStorage. No server, no account. Export your tuned weights & note any time.
